Feature Overview

Built for Modern Cities

Every feature in FixEveryCity is designed to bridge the gap between citizens and city administration — faster, smarter, and more transparently than ever.

01

🧠 Smart AI Responses

Natural language processing analyzes citizen descriptions and automatically extracts location, issue type, urgency level, and relevant department — no forms to fill.

NLP Auto-classify GPT-Powered
02

📡 Real-Time Analysis

Live data streams from field workers, IoT sensors, and citizen reports are aggregated and analyzed in real-time to give administrators a live pulse of the city.

Live Data Streaming IoT Ready
03

💡 Intelligent Suggestions

The AI cross-references your issue against 10,000+ past cases and suggests the most effective resolution path, saving departments hours of manual research.

Pattern Match History Search RAG
04

📊 Data Insights & Analytics

Powerful dashboards reveal issue hotspots, departmental KPIs, resolution velocities, and citizen satisfaction scores — all in one place.

Analytics Charts KPI Tracking
05

🔔 Smart Notifications

Citizens receive proactive updates via email, SMS, or push — including ETA changes, department hand-offs, and resolution confirmations.

Multi-channel Webhooks
06

🗺️ Geo-Aware Routing

Automatic geofencing assigns issues to the right local zone, ward, or district team regardless of how vaguely the citizen described the location.

GIS Geofencing Geo-AI
