How FixEveryCity Works

Three simple steps from issue discovery to resolution — powered by AI at every stage.

1

Report the Issue

Upload a photo or describe the problem. Our AI instantly understands context, location, and severity.

2

AI Routes & Prioritizes

The system assigns the ticket to the correct department and generates an estimated resolution timeline.

3

Track & Resolve

Follow progress in real-time. Get notified when your issue is resolved and rate the outcome.

4

Improve Together

Resolution data feeds back into our AI, making the system smarter with every fixed problem.
