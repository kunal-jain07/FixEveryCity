About FixEveryCity

We believe every citizen deserves a city that listens, responds, and improves — powered by AI and driven by community.

"Our mission is to eliminate the friction between citizens and city services — making urban governance faster, fairer, and driven by real data."
— The FixEveryCity Team, Hackathon 2026
🎯 The Challenge

Built in 5 Hours for Civic Impact

FixEveryCity was conceived and built during Hackathon 2026 in response to the challenge: "How can AI improve quality of life in urban environments?" We chose to tackle the gap between citizens with problems and municipalities with solutions.

🏙️ Civic Tech Track 🤖 AI/ML Category ⏱️ 5-Hour Sprint 🌍 Social Impact 🏆 Hackathon 2026
